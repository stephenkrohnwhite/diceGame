MODfarkle

    MODfarkle is played by rolling 4,6,8,10,12 and 14 sided dice.

    It can be played by 2 or more players. Each player rolls the dice and earns a score based on their roll. They roll 10 times to get a final score. The highest final score wins! Scoring is as follows:


    1. A straight, which must use all 6 dice, earns a score of 25,0000
    2. A six of a kind earns 20,000
    3. Two triples earns 17,500
    4. A four of a kind plus a pair earns 15,000
    5. Three pairs earns 12,500
    6. Other multiples earn a score of the number of occurences multiplied by 1000 and added to the dice value multiplied by 100.

    Get ready to play!
